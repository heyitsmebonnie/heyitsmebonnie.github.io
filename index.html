Introduction

The business of Vasati was built by four students as an assignment: Bonnie, Tiana, Tara and Taryn. We thought of the name Vasati after 2 other failed attempts as the name were taken, but we finally settled on this name and in ancient Persian it’s meaning is ‘to live’ or ‘dwell’. The meaning behind the comma after our name in our logo, is that we want to provide products that make you feel more than the feeling of ‘dwelling’ or only ‘living’, but we want to provide the illusion of a spa right in your own home. Our goal is to make high end relaxation, soothing and vitamin and mineral packed products without the massive price tag.

Vasati was founded primarily on the aim to provide consumers with 100% natural and handmade home spa items. We range from candles to soap and bath bombs and are unbeatable prices! A lot of companies are unable to prove that they use natural products to make their items and that they are handmade, while we are quite the opposite and we are determined to help the environment with our products.
